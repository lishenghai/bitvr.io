The Market and Problems

The Diverse Potential of VR & AR Applications

Non-entertainment VR/AR market is not growing fast enough. The total market potential is more than $35 billion but currently only 10% usage in entertainment. There is huge mass market demand for a wide range of VR material. However, the market is lack of content to support the growth because of:

    Upfront Cost is High

  • Because there is no trust between creators/contributors of production parties within the industry, therefore they need to guaranteed income before committing to create contents to put into the market.
  • High learning curve to create and produce, due to the nature of the creation process and hardware/software cost.

    No Guarantee in Return

  • High learning curve to create and produce, due to the nature of the creation process and hardware/software cost.
  • Copyright infringement has always been a disruptive issue.
  • Creators don’t have say of the pricing in marketplace.
  • Not transparent of where the content has been used.

    Difficult to Sell

  • Lack of marketplace.
  • There are only a few dominated marketplaces for non-entertainment VR/360/AR contents for commercial usage.

The Solutions

In order to activate the VR/360/AR commercial content market, we are going to apply a new business model leveraging the characteristics of blockchain, establish a decentralized eco-system that:

    Reduce Upfront Cost

  • Contributor layering profit share model - a blockchain base trusted platform that allows various contributors share the income automatically.
  • Tools to enable simple production
  • Academy and hardware/software rental and sponsorship

    Provide a trustable system that track the usage of the content and automatically distribute the revenue to show transparency.

  • Built on top of Blockchain
  • Contributor model and smart contract

    An effective marketplaceto trade VR contents

  • Content portals
  • Topic polling
  • Decentralize marketplace with trusted, fast and scalable payment form

BitVR Key Features

Layering Profit Share Model

To create a fair, transparent and decentralized reward models, bringing together different levels of content creators and customers.

Copyright Management System

The blockchain-based intellectual property right management system allows to openly verify content authorship and provenance. The smart contract monitors copyright compliance and automates bureaucratic processes.

Content data distributed in a decentralized storage system to generate high efficiency, secure and lower operation costs.

The Roadmap

Q3 2018 PRE-SALE

In this phase BitVR tokens will be sold to private investors before the actual crowd sale begins. Tokens sold at this stage will be allocated and distributed after the token creation event.

Q1 2019 ICO CROWDSALE

The public crowd sale will distribute tokens created at the token creation event through a crowd sale smart contract. Tokens not sold in the crowd sale will be added to the token vault. However, a small percentage will be retained for the BitVR airdrop.

Q1 2019 PARTNERSHIPS

Also, in this timeframe the first partnerships with content providers and VR hardware suppliers will have been created. In addition, third-party partnerships, aimed a leveraging the third-party API will be formed.

Q2 2019 BITBR AIR DROP

An airdrop will distribute additional tokens to BitVR token holders. This will incentivize investors to hold onto their tokens instead of selling after the ICO end. The source for the tokens distributed via airdrop will be the reserve tokens.

Q2 2019 BITVR VERSION 1.0

The BitVR system will see its first fully functional release with version 1.0.

This release will include:

  • The full BitVR web interface, including the BitVR token wallet. Users will have the ability to upload and consume VR content. The token economy will be enabled, allowing creators to monetize their content.

  • A centralized version of the content index database will allow searching for content and browsing by categories.

  • Content will be stored on a centralized file storage system in version 1, ready for later migration to decentralized storage.

  • In parallel the BitVR Academy will be launched, providing training content and tutorials on platform usage. In addition community meet-ups will be held to create platform awareness.

Q4 2019 BITVR VERSION 2.0

BitVR Version 2 will be released as an incremental update to version 1, including, introducing mainly IPR management facilities:

  • The IPR management modules will allow registering and protecting copyright of original content using the underlying blockchain.

  • Content will be represent on-chain in the form of an ERC-721 content token, integrating with IPR management and allowing ownership transfers. This will also enable decentralized content indexing, replacing the centralized database .

Q2 2020 BitVR Version 3.0

Rollout concludes with BitVR Version 3, which provides the full BitVR fundtionality:

  • Storage will be migrated to the decentralized file storage, removing the last major centralized component.

  • The third-party JavaScript integration library will enable third party content businesses to use the BitVR platform to manage and store content and embed it into their website.
